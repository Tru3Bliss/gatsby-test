What are the 4 rarity tiers and boost percentages?

Common: 0, Uncommon: +2.5%, Epic: +10% and Legendary: +25%.

Can I sell my warrior during the tournament or is it locked until the end?

You can sell your warrior at any time, just be sure to claim any $RROK in your account before you do.

What happens to my warrior if he is defeated?

Your warrior is no longer eligible to continue in battle, however, you may use $RROK to reincarnate for a discounted price of 50,000 $RROK which grants you 1 free mint in the next season.
